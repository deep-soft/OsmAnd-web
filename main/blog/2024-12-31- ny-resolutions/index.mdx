Happy New Year from OsmAnd!

As we step into 2025, we celebrate the tradition of reflecting on our journey and sharing our vision for the year ahead. This marks the seventh time we’ve come together to outline our milestones and aspirations, driven by our unwavering commitment to making your navigation smarter, simpler, and more reliable.

2024 was a transformative year, filled with advancements that enhanced the OsmAnd experience for millions of users worldwide. Looking forward, 2025 promises even more—innovative features, improved usability, and solutions designed with you in mind.

Ready to see how we’re planning to shape the future of navigation? Let’s dive in.

<!--truncate-->

## 2025 New Year Resolutions

### Simplified and Smarter User Experience

We’re committed to making OsmAnd more intuitive and efficient:

- Redesigning the UI for seamless navigation across Android, iOS, and the web.
- Fixing key bugs to ensure every tap and click works effortlessly.


### Intelligent Route Planning and Exploration

Elevate your adventures with advanced tools:

- Integrated weather updates to plan safer, smoother journeys.
- AI assistants to guide activity selection and optimize your route.  


### Collaboration and Community Sharing

Navigating together has never been easier:

- New public group features for shared experiences and collaboration.  
- A modernized tracking tool replacing OsmAnd Tracker for better real-time updates.  
- Multi-user collaborative editing in groups.  


### Quick Actions, Smarter Widgets, and Trip Computer

Your tools, your way:

- Fully customizable widgets and quick actions for personalized control.
- Enhanced trip computer to track your journey like never before, on iOS and Android. 


### Green Navigation for Tomorrow

Paving the way for eco-friendly travel:

- Routes optimized for CO2 savings, energy efficiency, and travel time.
- Dedicated features for electric vehicles, including charging stations.  
- Enhanced compatibility with Android Auto and CarPlay.  


### Reimagining Map Interaction and Search tool

Get a clearer picture of the world:

- Realistic 3D terrain views and updated road attributes.
- Advanced search features with heatmaps for GPX tracks.


### Smartwatch and External Device Support

Stay connected, wherever you are:

- Expanded support for smartwatches and external devices on iOS and Android.


### Improved Management of GPX Tracks and Favorites

We’re improving the way you organize your adventures:

- Enhanced GPX track handling for smooth navigation.  
- Advanced tools for favorites, especially on iOS.  


### Smart Assistance, Strong Documentation, and Guides

We’re here to help, every step of the way:

- Comprehensive guides for web and app functionalities.
- AI-powered support to make OsmAnd even more accessible.


### Other Innovations on the Horizon

We’re never short on ideas! Here’s a sneak peek:

- Finding better ways to get more people involved in the OpenStreetMap project.
- Creating smarter documentation and app guides.
- Exploring new ways for AI to enhance the OsmAnd experience.
- Boosting app performance for faster loading and smoother transitions.


## **2024: A Year of Transformation**  

In 2024, OsmAnd reached new milestones that reshaped the way you navigate the world. Here’s a glimpse of how we turned resolutions into reality, bringing innovation and usability to every corner of our app.

[4.7](https://osmand.net/blog/osmand-ios-4-7-released), [4.8](https://osmand.net/blog/osmand-ios-4-8-released), [4.9](https://osmand.net/blog/osmand-ios-4-9-released) **(iOS)**; [4.7](https://osmand.net/blog/osmand-android-4-7-released), [4.8](https://osmand.net/blog/osmand-android-4-8-released), [4.9](https://osmand.net/blog/osmand-android-4-9-released) **(Android)**, [1.01](https://osmand.net/blog/osmand-web-1-01-released/) Map **Web**.

[Resolutions 2024](https://osmand.net/blog/ny-resolutions-2024):

- ✔️ **Customizable quick actions and widgets**. Effortlessly tailor your navigation tools. Custom and default buttons, quick actions for the external keyboard - each feature is designed to meet your unique needs.
- ✔️ **Enhanced map visualizations**. Experience the world in greater detail with redesigned menus, weather animations, and specialized maps like dirt bike trails.
- ✔️ **Smarter Routing and Navigation**. With features like fast offline routing, auto-zoom, and new widgets, your journeys have never been smoother or more efficient.
- ✔️ **Visual Exploration with Photos**. Dive into rich visual content with Wikimedia gallery viewers for both web and Android.
